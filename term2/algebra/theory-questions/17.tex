$\R, f = x^2 + 1$.
$$\R[x]_{/(x^2 + 1)} = \{a + b[x] \mid a, b \in \R\}$$ 
$$(a + b[x])(c + d[x]) = $$ 
$$= ac + (ad + bx)[x] + bd[x^2] =$$ 
$$= ac + (ad + bc)[x] + bd[-1] = $$
$$= ac - bd + (ad + bc)[x] \text{ "--- напоминает умножение в $\C$} $$
$$\R[x]_{/(x^2 + 1)} \rightarrow \C$$
$$a + b[x] \rightarrow a + bi$$
